Instagram

Rule of Thirds

Instagram.com

The Instagram website uses the rule of thirds to frame images and make them look more attractive. On the camera screen, for example, a grid is displayed that divides the screen into nine equal parts to help users apply the rule of thirds when taking their photos

Google

Hick's Law

Google.com

The Google search engine uses Hick's Law to simplify the user experience when searching for information online. Instead of displaying all available search options on a single screen, the site uses an algorithm that ranks search results based on the relevance and authority of the website providing it.

LinkedIn

Visual Hierarchy

LinkedIn.com

The LinkedIn home page uses visual hierarchy to guide the user through the page and highlight important information. The user's personal information, such as their name and title, is presented in bold at the top of the page to attract the user's attention, while additional content is presented in a structured, easy-to-follow layout.
